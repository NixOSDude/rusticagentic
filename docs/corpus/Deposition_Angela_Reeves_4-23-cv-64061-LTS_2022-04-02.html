Deposition — Angela Reeves

DEPOSITION TRANSCRIPT


Case: Redwood Software Group Inc. v. Halcyon Data Systems Corp.

Case No.: 6-23-cv-64382-LTS

Court: United States District Court

Witness: Angela Reeves, Chief People Officer

Date: 2022-04-02

Examining Attorney: [Counsel for Halcyon Data Systems Corp.], Sullivan & Cromwell LLP


EXAMINATION BY COUNSEL:


Q: Please state your name and current title for the record.

A: My name is Angela Reeves. I serve as Chief People Officer.


Q: How long have you been in that role?

A: Approximately 9 years.


Q: I want to direct your attention to the subject of trade secret access and disclosure.

Were you personally involved in those matters?

A: Yes, I was directly involved.


Q: Please describe your involvement.

A: I oversaw the relevant process and was responsible for ensuring compliance

with all applicable agreements and obligations.


Q: Were you aware of the specific contractual provisions at issue?

A: Yes. I reviewed those provisions carefully before proceeding.


Q: Did you take any steps to comply with the notice requirements?

A: We followed our standard procedures, which I believed to be compliant.


Q: I'm showing you what has been marked as Exhibit A. Do you recognize this document?

A: Yes, I signed it. I understood it restricted certain activities.


Q: And you understood the geographic scope of that restriction?

A: Yes. I believe it covered the continental United States.


Q: At any point did you consult legal counsel regarding these obligations?

A: Yes, we engaged Sullivan & Cromwell LLP to advise us on compliance.


Q: Nothing further. Thank you.
