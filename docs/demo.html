Before Your Live Demo

We run the demo on your documents.
Not ours. That's the point.

The most compelling demonstration is the one where you recognize the documents. To make every minute count — and to protect your time and ours — we ask that you prepare the following before booking your session.

⚠
Use Sample or Redacted Documents Only

Do not submit live client files, privileged matter documents, or any materials covered by attorney-client privilege or confidentiality obligations. Use sanitized, redacted, or synthetic versions of representative documents. A demo corpus of anonymized contracts — party names replaced, deal terms generalized — is entirely sufficient to demonstrate the system's capabilities and does not implicate your professional responsibility obligations or BAR ethics rules. If you are unsure whether a document is appropriate to share, it is not.

01
Document Volume
50 to 150 documents

Enough to make retrieval non-trivial — answers that cite four different contracts from four different counterparties are far more convincing than a single-document lookup. More than 200 documents adds ingest time without adding demo impact. The sweet spot is 75–120 documents across three or more document types.

02
File Formats Accepted
PDF · DOC · DOCX · TXT · MD · RST · CSV · HTML · PNG · JPG · JPEG · TIFF · BMP · WEBP
PDF — text-searchable preferred; scanned PDFs supported via OCR
DOCX / DOC — Microsoft Word, any version
TXT / MD / RST / CSV / HTML — native text, no extraction required
PNG / JPG / JPEG / TIFF / BMP / WEBP — full image OCR
Mixed formats in a single corpus are fine — and preferred.
OCR disclaimer: Optical character recognition is applied to scanned PDFs, images, and handwritten documents. OCR output is an approximation — accuracy depends on scan quality, resolution, handwriting legibility, and document condition. Handwritten notes in particular may be partially or incorrectly transcribed and may not fully or accurately reflect the source document. Faded text, low-resolution scans, skewed pages, stamps, and redactions can all reduce accuracy. Always verify OCR-extracted content against the original source document before relying on it for any legal, factual, or evidentiary purpose.
03
Delivery & Sensitivity
24 hrs before · GCS upload link

Once your demo is confirmed, we provision a dedicated Google Cloud Storage bucket scoped to your session and send you a signed upload link. Drop your documents in — no account required, no third-party service. We ingest, index, and verify the system before the call — zero setup time on your clock.

The bucket is isolated to your demo session. All documents and derived indexes are permanently deleted immediately after the session ends — we confirm deletion in writing. No copies, no embeddings, no residual data retained.

Prepare Exactly 10 Queries

The questions your associates have actually spent hours researching — the ones where you pulled every contract in a matter to find one clause. Bring those 10. We run them live against your documents and return an answer with source citations in seconds. The closer they are to real work, the more compelling the result. Ten queries is the right number — enough variety to be definitive, focused enough to stay within 30 minutes.

Formulate for the Answer You Want to See

Don't ask "What do the NDAs say?" — ask "Which NDAs permit disclosure to affiliates, and under what conditions?" Specific questions produce specific, citable answers. Think about the exact outcome you want to demonstrate to your partners — then work backwards to the question that surfaces it. The system is only as compelling as the queries you bring.
