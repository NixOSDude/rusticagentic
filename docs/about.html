About RusticAgentic

Built on a principle.
Sharpened over decades.

RusticAgentic exists because law firms deserve AI that works for them — not AI that works with their clients' secrets. Here is our mission, our story, and the values we will not compromise.

Our Mission

Law firms handle some of the most sensitive documents in existence — privileged communications, M&A strategy, deposition transcripts, settlement terms. Today, searching that material means uploading it to a third party. LexisNexis. Relativity. Harvey. Every one of them takes custody of your clients' secrets to give you an answer.

RusticAgentic exists to end that trade-off.

Our mission is to give law firms the power of AI document intelligence without surrendering control of their data. Every query runs on infrastructure the firm owns — a dedicated private cloud node or on-premises hardware. Documents are encrypted at rest with post-quantum cryptography. Every access is logged with a tamper-evident, cryptographically signed audit trail. Nothing leaves. Nothing is shared. Nothing is stored on our servers, because we don't have any.

We believe attorney-client privilege should extend to the tools attorneys use — not just the conversations they have. That is the principle RusticAgentic was built on, and it is the only principle we will not compromise.

Beyond the technology, we are committed to our clients. We deliver enterprise-grade AI infrastructure that simplifies workflows, surfaces answers in seconds, and eliminates the friction that slows legal teams down. We stand behind every deployment — fully available, fully accountable. Our goal is not to hand you a product and disappear. It is to make your practice faster, more informed, and more capable than it was before.

Our Story

Scott Baker's relationship with computing started in the 1980s — summer school at Haverford Preparatory School, a Mac SE/30, and HyperCard. That early instinct for building interactive systems never left.

In the early 2000s he ran his own architectural design firm, working closely with attorneys on contracts, agreements, and legal documentation. He watched them rely on LexisNexis to search and retrieve case law and legal records — expensive, cloud-dependent, and entirely outside the firm's control. That memory stayed with him.

When he began building a Rust-based RAG system years later and looked for the right market to apply it, legal firms came immediately to mind. He knew the problem firsthand: lawyers need fast, accurate document search across sensitive privileged material — and every existing solution requires sending that material to someone else's servers. That gap is what RusticAgentic was built to close.

Since late 2022, long-term disability has ended Scott's ability to work onsite. Rather than stepping back from engineering, he stepped all the way in. With nothing but time and the discipline to use it, he has spent those years in a level of technical immersion that a conventional career simply does not permit — mastering Rust, C23, CUDA, post-quantum cryptography, distributed systems, cloud architecture, and AI pipeline engineering from first principles.

The result is RusticAgentic — a platform that could only be built by someone who understood the client's problem from the inside, had the time to own every layer, and had the discipline to build it right. No external AI APIs. No shared infrastructure. No data egress. Just a system that does exactly what it claims, provably, on hardware the client controls.

Our Values
Sovereignty First

Your data never leaves your infrastructure — ever. We build systems where the client holds every key, owns every byte, and can verify every claim independently.

No Compromise on Privacy

Attorney-client privilege extends to the tools attorneys use. We will never build a feature that requires your documents to touch shared infrastructure.

Transparency & Accountability

Every access is logged. Every deliverable is cryptographically signed. Every audit trail is tamper-evident. We build for verifiability, not trust-me.

Engineering Depth

We own every layer — the cryptography, the embedding pipeline, the vector index, the inference server. No wrappers, no SaaS dependencies, no black boxes.

Client Commitment

We stand behind every deployment. Fully available, fully accountable. We are not a product you buy and figure out alone — we are here for the long run.

Future-Proof Security

Post-quantum cryptography today, not when it becomes an emergency. ML-KEM + ML-DSA — NIST FIPS 203/204 — built in from day one so your vault is safe against the threats that are coming.

All RusticAgentic deliverables are post-quantum signed with ML-DSA-65 (NIST FIPS 204).
